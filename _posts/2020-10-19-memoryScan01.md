## Intro
Not too long ago, a friend sent me message about a game we used to play at school and was shut down but recently brought back to live. This game's name was Tales of Pirates (now renamed as Pirate King Online). I felt nostalgic and tempted to play it but I also was hesitant as I know this MMORPG games usually demand lots of time for things like leveling, farming for resources and making money. Since time is gold as they say, I decided to write some python scripts to help me do the boring stuff for me.
